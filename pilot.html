Become a Fighter Pilot

Interested in joining the 134th Fighter Squadron?

"I appreciate your interest in becoming an aviator with the Virtual 158th Fighter Wing. When preparing your application, please avoid using telling us about just your flying stats. Save your time we are primarily interested in the facts. The pilot screening board wants to know why you would make a great officer and aviator.

"As a fighter pilot, you travel worldwide, sometimes to challenging environments. You are responsible for both taking and saving lives. Sometimes, you are sleep-deprived, stressed, and lonely; however, at the end of the day, you experience a profound sense of meaning and purpose.

"It's important to understand that the Virtual Vermont Air National Guard is not a flying club. We are a dedicated military organization providing airpower to the Combatant Commanders. If you are ready for this challenge, I look forward to meeting you."

vCol Marceline "Smore", 134th FS CC
