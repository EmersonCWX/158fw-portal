Become a Fighter Pilot

Interested in joining the v158th Fighter Wing?

Greetings! I'm deeply humbled in your interest in becoming part of the crew with the Virtual 158th Fighter Wing. We are a tight-knit community with a drive to have fun, foster a learning environment, and share a common interest in military aviation. We're excited to get to know you.

When preparing your application, we want to know about you as an individual, rather than your statistics or flying abilities. We want to know about your interest, and why you'd like to be an addition to our squadrons.

Above all, though, we're looking for people who share enthusiasm, keep their chin up, and enjoy working as a team. If that sounds familiar, I look forward to meeting you.

vCol. Marceline "Smore" J., v158th Fighter Wing, Vice Commander
