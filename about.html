About the Virtual 158th Fighter Squadron

The Virtual 158th Fighter Squadron, known as the "Green Mountain Boys," represents excellence in flightsim military aviation. With a proud heritage, we continue to uphold the highest standards of professionalism, dedication, and tactical superiority.

Based in South Burlington, Vermont, our squadron is committed to maintaining air superiority and protecting American interests at home and abroad. Our pilots and personnel are trained to the highest standards and equipped with cutting-edge aircraft and technology.
